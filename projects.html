GraphiStasis

Used MLP and GNNs (SAGE, GCN, GAT) to predict epistatic interactions using a bipartite gene-disease graph.

Tested the models' viability on a set of 108 schizophrenia genes identified by a 2014 GWAS and evaluated embedding space validity using PCA.

Currently exploring follow-up analyses and different gene embeddings.

Tools: PyTorch Geometric, PyTorch, Biopython Entrez, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib
